April 2023 - September 2023

The supervisor was Paris Avgeriou and Darius Sas from the University of Groningen as well as Ioannis Stamelos from the Aristotle University of Thessaloniki. In particular, the thesis implements a well-known metric among software engineers, Parent Centrality, inside an existing tool, ASTracker. Octopus

November 2020 - January 2021

The "Octopus" application is an incident management system designed with a focus on meeting the needs of small businesses. Within this system, every user is assigned to one or more groups, enabling them to access specific capabilities within the program. Moreover, users are empowered to execute various actions, contingent on their assigned privileges, including the following:

  • Incident and Request Management (Submission, Case Monitoring, Completion).
  • Statistics Monitoring.
  • Chatting between Users.
  • Group and Privileges Creation.
  • Log Files Surveillance.

It is offered as SaaS and was created using the "Laravel" programming language for advanced security.

The application was built in collaboration with Nikolaos Manoukidis
